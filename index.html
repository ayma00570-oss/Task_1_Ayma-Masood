Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Lahore Since 2003

Founded on Jail Road, Lahore, CarePoint Hospital was built on one simple idea — every family deserves quality healthcare. Today we are one of Punjab's most trusted multi-specialty hospitals.

From routine checkups to complex surgeries, our team is here for every step of your health journey.

  • ✔ PMDC & Punjab Healthcare Commission Certified
  • ✔ Robotic Surgery & Laparoscopy Unit
  • ✔ 24-hour Pharmacy & Lab Services
  • ✔ Dedicated ICU, NICU & Trauma Centre
